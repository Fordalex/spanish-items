Present Tense (Presente)

Used for actions happening now or regularly.

Example: Yo como (I eat).

Preterite Tense (Pretérito)

Used for completed actions in the past.

Example: Yo comí (I ate).

Imperfect Tense (Imperfecto)

Used for ongoing or habitual actions in the past.

Example: Yo comía (I used to eat).

Future Tense (Futuro)

Used for actions that will happen.

Example: Yo comeré (I will eat).
